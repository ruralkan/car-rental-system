# Car Rental System - Interview Questions
## Car Management Role (Service, Delivery, Pickup)

### Vehicle Inventory Management
1. How many vehicles do you plan to start with, and what is the growth projection?
2. What vehicle categories/types will you offer (economy, compact, sedan, SUV, premium, specialty)?
3. How should vehicle information be tracked (VIN, license plate, model year, mileage, current location)?
4. Should the system track vehicle status (available, rented, in maintenance, reserved)?
5. What vehicle details are important for rental selection (passenger capacity, luggage capacity, features)?

### Service & Maintenance Management
6. What maintenance schedules need to be tracked (regular service, oil changes, inspections)?
7. How should maintenance requests be logged and prioritized?
8. Should the system track service history and associated costs for each vehicle?
9. How are maintenance vendors managed (internal workshop or external service providers)?
10. What notifications are needed for upcoming maintenance (service due warnings, inspection reminders)?

### Damage & Condition Tracking
11. How should pre-rental vehicle condition be documented (photos, checklists, damage reports)?
12. What damage assessment process should the system support?
13. How should damage findings be communicated to customers?
14. Should the system estimate repair costs and track liability?
15. How are damage-related charges assigned to customers?
16. What documentation is needed for insurance claims?

### Vehicle Pickup & Delivery
17. What delivery and pickup locations are supported (airport, city center, hotel, customer location)?
18. Should the system support home delivery/pickup service?
19. How should delivery schedules be coordinated?
20. What is the process for vehicle handover and documentation at pickup?
21. Should vehicle keys and access cards be tracked?
22. How are delivery costs calculated and tracked?

### Vehicle Return & Inspection
23. What is the vehicle return process (customer returns to location, driver picks up)?
24. How should return condition be inspected and documented?
25. Should the system capture mileage, fuel level, and damages at return?
26. How should excessive wear or damage be flagged and reported?
27. What photo/video documentation is needed at return?
28. How long is the post-return grace period before final charges are confirmed?

### Fuel Management
29. What fuel policies should be supported (customer fills up, prepaid fuel, fuel surcharges)?
30. How should fuel consumption be tracked across the fleet?
31. Should the system calculate fuel charges based on mileage or fuel consumption?
32. How are fuel prices updated in the system?
33. What are the procedures for handling fuel discrepancies?

### Fleet Utilization & Optimization
34. What metrics should be tracked (utilization rate, average rental duration, revenue per vehicle)?
35. How should the system help with vehicle positioning across locations?
36. Should the system predict demand and recommend vehicle reallocation?
37. How are vehicle rotation and rest periods managed?
38. What reports are needed for fleet performance analysis?

### Reservation & Booking Management
39. How are available vehicles determined and displayed to customers?
40. Should the system support vehicle selection preferences (automatic transmission, specific model)?
41. What blocking/hold procedures exist for vehicle maintenance or availability issues?
42. How should overbooking situations be handled?
43. What are the upgrade/downgrade policies when requested vehicles are unavailable?

### Equipment & Accessories
44. What additional equipment can be added to rentals (GPS, child seats, roof rack, phone chargers)?
45. How should equipment inventory be tracked separately from vehicles?
46. Should the system handle damage to equipment and associated charges?
47. What is the process for equipment pickup and return?

### Documentation & Compliance
48. What documents are required for vehicle rental (rental agreement, driver's license verification)?
49. How should rental agreements be generated and stored?
50. What regulatory compliance is needed (emissions, safety inspections, registration)?
51. How are recalls and safety issues tracked and communicated?
52. Should the system support electronic signatures for rental documents?
