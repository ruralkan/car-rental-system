# Car Rental System - Interview Questions
## Risk Role

### Customer Risk Assessment
1. What criteria should be used to assess customer creditworthiness?
2. Should the system implement driver risk profiling based on age, driving history, or other factors?
3. How should high-risk bookings be flagged or require additional verification?
4. What identity verification methods are required (government ID, credit check, background check)?
5. Should the system integrate with external risk databases or credit bureaus?

### Insurance & Liability Management
6. What insurance coverage options should be offered (basic, comprehensive, premium)?
7. How is liability determined when accidents occur?
8. What is the process for handling insurance claims?
9. Should the system track driver insurance status or requirements?
10. How should uninsured/underinsured incidents be managed?
11. What documentation is required for insurance purposes?

### Vehicle Risk & Loss Prevention
12. What measures prevent vehicle theft (GPS tracking, immobilizers, geofencing)?
13. How should the system detect and alert on unauthorized vehicle movement?
14. What GPS or telematics data should be collected from vehicles?
15. How are stolen vehicles tracked and reported?
16. What insurance claims procedures exist for theft or loss?

### Damage & Loss Management
17. How is damage responsibility determined (customer vs. accident vs. pre-existing)?
18. What damage thresholds trigger investigation or disputes?
19. How are damage costs estimated and validated?
20. What is the dispute resolution process for damage claims?
21. Should the system track patterns of damage by drivers or locations?

### Fraud Detection & Prevention
22. What fraud scenarios should the system detect (false damage claims, identity fraud, payment fraud)?
23. How should suspicious bookings or transactions be flagged?
24. What authentication measures prevent unauthorized account access?
25. How are fraudulent refund requests detected and handled?
26. What is the process for reporting fraud to relevant authorities?

### Driver Safety & Compliance
27. Should the system verify driver license validity and status?
28. How are driving restrictions or suspensions checked?
29. What age-based restrictions exist (minimum rental age, minimum insurance age)?
30. How is international driver documentation validated?
31. Should the system track traffic violations or accidents by drivers?

### Rental Agreement & Legal Protection
32. How comprehensive should the rental agreement terms be?
33. What liability waivers should be included in the rental agreement?
34. How are rental agreements signed and stored (digital signatures, paper)?
35. What legal jurisdiction applies to rental disputes?
36. Should the system support multiple agreement templates by region or customer type?

### Financial Risk Management
37. What is the maximum liability exposure per vehicle or per incident?
38. How is financial exposure calculated and tracked?
39. Should the system implement credit limits per customer?
40. What deposit requirements exist to mitigate financial risk?
41. How are outstanding charges collected from high-risk customers?

### Geolocation & Boundary Risks
42. Should the system enforce geographic boundaries for vehicle use (geofencing)?
43. How should off-boundary vehicle movement be detected and handled?
44. Are there restricted or high-risk geographic areas?
45. Should customers be restricted from certain locations?
46. How are boundary violations reported and addressed?

### Accident & Incident Management
47. What is the incident reporting process (customer self-reporting, automated detection, police report)?
48. How should accident severity be assessed?
49. What documentation is required (photos, witness information, police report)?
50. Should the system support immediate emergency notifications?
51. How are warranty claims vs. customer-responsible damages determined?

### Regulatory Compliance & Monitoring
52. What regulations apply to car rental operations in your jurisdiction?
53. Are there data protection requirements (GDPR, CCPA) for customer information?
54. What audit trails are required for regulatory compliance?
55. How should regulatory violations or non-compliance be tracked?
56. What reporting is needed for regulatory authorities?

### Third-Party & Vendor Risk
57. If using service providers (maintenance, delivery), how is vendor performance monitored?
58. What insurance or indemnification agreements are needed with service providers?
59. How are vendor incidents or failures tracked and resolved?
60. Should the system implement vendor risk scoring?

### Data Security & Privacy Risk
61. What data security standards must be met (encryption, access controls)?
62. How is customer personal data protected against breaches?
63. What are the procedures for data breach notification?
64. How long should customer data be retained?
65. What systems are in place for secure data disposal?
