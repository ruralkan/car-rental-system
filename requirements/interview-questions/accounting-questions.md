# Car Rental System - Interview Questions
## Accounting Role

### Financial Planning & Budgeting
1. What is the planned budget allocation for the car rental business line?
2. How do you want to track revenue streams (vehicle rental, insurance, fuel surcharges, late fees)?
3. What cost categories need to be tracked (vehicle acquisition, maintenance, insurance, fuel, staff)?
4. What financial KPIs are most important (profitability per vehicle, revenue per booking, operating margin)?

### Pricing & Revenue Management
5. How should pricing be structured (daily rates, weekly rates, monthly rates, hourly rates)?
6. Do you need dynamic pricing based on demand, seasonality, or vehicle type?
7. How should ancillary charges be managed (insurance, GPS, child seats, fuel surcharges)?
8. What discount policies need to be reflected in the accounting system (corporate discounts, loyalty discounts, volume discounts)?

### Invoice & Billing Management
9. What invoicing processes are needed (automatic invoicing at pickup, invoicing at return, post-rental invoicing)?
10. Should the system support different billing arrangements (per-rental, monthly billing for corporate customers, subscription models)?
11. How should deposits and damage charges be handled and accounted for?
12. What tax considerations need to be built into the system (sales tax, VAT, regional tax differences)?

### Payment Processing & Financial Controls
13. What payment methods need to be accepted (credit cards, debit cards, cash, bank transfers, digital wallets)?
14. Should the system support partial payments or payment plans?
15. How should refunds and credits be processed and tracked?
16. What financial reconciliation processes are needed between the booking system and accounting records?

### Cost Management & Expense Tracking
17. How should vehicle maintenance costs be tracked and allocated?
18. What expense categories need detailed tracking (fuel, insurance, repairs, cleaning, registration)?
19. Should the system track cost per vehicle, per fleet, or by location?
20. How are depreciation and vehicle lifecycle costs managed?

### Financial Reporting & Auditing
21. What financial reports are required (profit & loss, cash flow, balance sheet, revenue by vehicle type)?
22. How frequently are financial reports needed (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly)?
23. What audit trail requirements exist for financial transactions?
24. Should the system integrate with existing accounting software (QuickBooks, SAP, NetSuite)?

### Multi-Location & Multi-Currency Support
25. If expanding to multiple locations, how should revenue and costs be allocated by location?
26. Do you need multi-currency support and currency conversion tracking?
27. How should inter-location transfers of vehicles be accounted for?

### Compliance & Regulatory
28. What financial compliance requirements exist (local tax laws, financial reporting standards)?
29. Are there specific documentation requirements for audits or regulatory bodies?
30. How long should financial transaction records be retained?
