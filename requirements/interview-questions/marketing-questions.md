# Car Rental System - Interview Questions
## Marketing Role

### Business Overview & Goals
1. What are the primary market segments you want to target (business travelers, leisure travelers, local customers, corporate accounts)?
2. What is your target market size and growth projection for the car rental service?
3. What are the key competitive advantages you want to emphasize in the market?
4. What is your pricing strategy compared to competitors (premium, economy, value-based)?

### Customer Acquisition & Retention
5. What channels do you plan to use for customer acquisition (direct website, travel agencies, corporate partnerships, mobile apps, OTA platforms)?
6. How do you plan to build customer loyalty and encourage repeat bookings?
7. What customer retention metrics are most important to track?
8. Do you have plans for a loyalty or rewards program?

### Customer Journey & Booking Experience
9. What should the ideal customer booking journey look like?
10. What information needs to be captured from customers during the booking process?
11. What booking options are needed (advance reservations, same-day bookings, corporate accounts)?
12. Should the system support multiple languages and currency options?
13. What are the key touchpoints where you want to communicate with customers?

### Promotions & Campaigns
14. How frequently do you plan to run promotional campaigns?
15. What types of discounts or offers do you want to support (percentage off, fixed amount, seasonal, loyalty-based)?
16. Do you need to track campaign effectiveness and ROI?
17. Should the system support promotional codes, coupons, or special corporate rates?

### Customer Communication
18. What communication channels do you want to use (email, SMS, app notifications, WhatsApp)?
19. What information should be sent to customers (confirmation, pickup details, reminders, offers)?
20. When should automated communications be triggered (booking confirmation, pickup reminder, post-rental survey)?

### Analytics & Reporting
21. What marketing metrics are critical for your business (booking conversion rate, customer acquisition cost, average booking value)?
22. What data do you need to generate marketing reports (daily bookings, customer demographics, regional performance)?
23. How often do you need access to marketing dashboards and reports?

### Brand & Market Positioning
24. How do you want to position your car rental brand in the market?
25. What unique selling points or differentiators should the system highlight to customers?
26. Are there specific vehicle categories or rental durations you want to emphasize?

### Integration with Sales Channels
27. Do you have existing customer databases that need to be integrated?
28. Should the system integrate with travel agency booking systems or corporate travel management platforms?
29. What third-party marketing tools do you currently use (CRM, email marketing, analytics)?
